Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I add a spot?

To add a new Spot simply follow these steps :

  1. Click the big Add a Spot button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up (if possible please add an image) and press submit.

2. How long is the map open for?

The map will be open for contributions until 24 June, 2016.

3. Do you delete any contributions?

All spots are subject to moderation from time to time to check for inappropriate content (spots may be edited or omitted).

4. What if I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to change something, email info@creativesuburbs.com and they'll help sort it out.

5. Do you have any tips on using the map and placing or supporting spots

Yes, if you click on the Layers button in the top left hand side of the map, you will be able to switch between the. You can easily find your location on the map using the different layers and move around using the mouse. Check that you have not repositioned the map after you have placed your spot.

Have a look at all the spots in your area by switching between List All Spots and Show the Map views by clicking on the box in the top right hand side. In the map view you can see all the spots or filter the spots. You can add comments to any of the spots, hover over the support button and click on it to add additional support. Spots can be shared on Facebook or Twitter.

6. How will my personal information be used?

Your privacy is important. The information being requested is collected by Creative Suburbs and it will be used and held in accordance with Creative Suburbs Privacy Policy. It may be used for the purpose of providing feedback results of this project, keeping in touch with you our latest news or upcoming projects and events. You may remain anonymous. If you do not provide information, we may not be able to provide you with certain information or assistance. If you do not want to receive updates, or to access or change your information, please contact us using the details set out in our Privacy Policy.
