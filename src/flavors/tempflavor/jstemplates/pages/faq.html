Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I add a place?

To add a new Place simply follow these steps :

  1. Click the big Add a Place button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up (if possible please add an image) and press submit.

2. How long is the map open for?

The map will be open for contributions until 24 June, 2016.

3. Do you delete any contributions?

All places are subject to moderation from time to time to check for inappropriate content (places may be edited or omitted).

4. What if I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to change something, email info@creativesuburbs.com and they'll help sort it out.

5. How will my personal information be used?

Your privacy is important. The information being requested is collected by Creative Suburbs and it will be used and held in accordance with Creative Suburbs Privacy Policy. It may be used for the purpose of providing feedback results of this project, keeping in touch with you our latest news or upcoming projects and events. You may remain anonymous. If you do not provide information, we may not be able to provide you with certain information or assistance. If you do not want to receive updates, or to access or change your information, please contact us using the details set out in our Privacy Policy.
