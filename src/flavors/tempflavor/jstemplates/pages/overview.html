Welcome!

Greening the Pipeline aims to transform the Main Outfall Sewer reserve into a linear public space that takes a more holistic approach to water management and enhances community livability by:

  • Improving the local amenity
  • Enhancing community connections & sense of place
  • Contributing to urban cooling
  • Enhancing active transport opportunities

It is one of the largest infrastructure projects of its kind in Australia and that's why we need you to provide your feedback to shape the project.

This project is open source
