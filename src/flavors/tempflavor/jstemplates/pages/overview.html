Greening the Pipeline aims to transform the Main Outfall Sewer reserve into a linear parkland that takes a more holistic approach to water management and enhances community livability by:

  • Improve health and wellbeing
  • Activate community sense of place
  • Enhance active transport links
  • Create resilient open space

The Greening the Pipeline initiative provides an opportunity to create a unique parkland. We need your feedback to help shape the design.
